Expert opinion

Bean Poet is a website that helps people make and enjoy better coffee. The world of coffee, specialty coffee in particular, can be intimidating to newcomers. You wont find any coffee snobs at Bean Poetbut if you want to become one, it isnt a bad place to start.

Expert :

Erik Rolfsen

Editor of Bean Poet

His comments

Monica

Decaf Cappuccino

If anyone needs to stick to decaf, it's Monica. She's pretty high-strung, and it almost frightens me to think how high her stress levels would hit if it were also powered by caffeine.

Still, she's a good friend and if you ever needed somebody to talk to over a warm, frothy beverage, she'd be there for you.

Rachel

Decaf Cappuccino

As the fashionista of the group, Rachel is probably the most discerning about the quality of her coffee. I have no doubt she'd prefer caffeine but chooses decaf for misguided health reasons.

It's the '90s, so the health benefits of caffeine aren't yet widely known. I could see Rachel making the switch to caffeinated at some point. She's not a morning person.

Phoebe

Decaf Cappuccino

Phoebe's no coffee snob. I don't think she cares much about the origin of her beans, the degree of the roast, or Gunther putting just the right tamp on her espresso.

She's easygoing enough to take whatever you serve her, but I'm sure she'd pick the highest quality coffee in a blind taste test, just by accident.

Chandler

Coffee black

Black coffee suits Chandler. He might be the least pretentious of the Friends characters, so it makes sense that his drink is no frills as well.

The caffeine won't help him with his nerves around women, but it will help him focus on his work as a data analyst. And Chandler is frugalhe's not going to drop any more cash than necessary on his morning joe.

Ross

Ice tea

Ross once mocked Chandler for drinking chocolate milk at age 29, so it's ironic that his go-to order is another child's drink. It's also a shame, because he'd make a great coffee geek.

Coffee brewing is science. Give Ross a home espresso machine and he'd spend hours tweaking variables to get just the right extraction.

Joey

Latte

Italians don't put milk in their coffee after noon, so Joey's all-hours embrace of the caffe latte tells us he's more new country than old.

The high milk content in a latte means it contains more protein and more fat than almost any other coffee drink, and since Joey is an eating machine, perhaps the latte is just his way of holding off the hunger until his next meal.
